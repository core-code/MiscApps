TableEdit-Lite

v1.4.3


Description:

TableEdit-Lite is the free version of the Mac-native spreadsheet TableEdit.


System Requirements:

• macOS 13 'Ventura' or later


License & Cost:

TableEdit-Lite is free and open-source.


Contact & Support:

The product website is located here: https://www.corecode.io/tableedit_lite/

Technical support is available only from within TableEdit-Lite, select "Contact support…" from the "Help" menu.


Usage:

0.) Download TableEdit-Lite from the "Mac App Store" or from its homepage and once it has finished downloading start it from Launchpad or your Applications folder.

1.) A new spreadsheet will be opened where you can enter your data. Click on a cell to enter data directly into the cell.

2.) You can increase the number of rows and columns in your spreadsheet by using the '+'-buttons embedded in the table or by using the 'Insert' menu items.


If you need any features not available, we suggest having a look at the full version, TableEdit.
