Q: What is the benefit of using 'VideoBreakout'?

A:

'VideoBreakout' allows you to view web-videos in a proper and native video-player instead of having to watch them embedded into a website in your browser. This allows for

• Higher quality and performance

• More convenient because the video is in its own window, can be freely moved around and resized instead of being embedded in a larger website-window

• Many more playback options like filters, playback speed, etc

In any case, if the thought that its inconvenient to watch a video that is embedded into a website, 'VideoBreakout' is probably not for you.



Q: Is it legal to use 'VideoBreakout'?

A:

'VideoBreakout' allows you to view web-videos without having to see the annoying ads on the website. Insofar, the effect of VideoBreakout is the same as an ad-blocker - and adblockers are legal. But some websites may have Terms of Service that disallows circumventing their ads or viewing their videos outside their website (which may or may not be legally binding in your country). We'd recommend you to check the Terms of the website and/or consult a lawyer if in doubt.
